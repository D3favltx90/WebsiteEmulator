Big Buck Bunny is a short computer-animated comedy film featuring animals of the forest, made by the Blender Institute, part of the Blender Foundation.

The plot follows a day in the life of Big Buck Bunny, during which time he meets three bullying rodents: Frank, Rinky, and Gamera. The rodents amuse themselves by harassing helpless creatures of the forest by throwing fruits, nuts, and rocks at them.
